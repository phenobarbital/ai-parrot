# Market Researcher Agents Summary

| Agent Name | Short Description (purpose extracted from the `system_prompt`) |
|-----------|---------------------------------------------------------------|
| **industry_overview** | *Industry Overview Specialist* – provides a comprehensive overview of an industry, covering definition, key characteristics, current state, recent developments, and industry evolution. |
| **services_data** | *Services Data Collector* – uses Google Search tools to find primary services (retailing, support, distribution, etc.) of a specific industry and reports market share and product/service explanations. |
| **market_data** | *Market Data Analyst* – researches market size, growth, trends, forecasts, and top‑5 company market‑share percentages, with brief competitive positioning. |
| **major_markets** | *Market Segmentation & Geography Analyst* – analyzes major markets across geographic, customer‑segment, channel, and vertical dimensions, providing detailed percentages and data. |
| **competitor_data** | *Competitive Intelligence Analyst* – conducts deep competitive analysis, profiling major competitors, their market‑share, revenue, geography, product portfolios, strategies, strengths/weaknesses, and recent activities. |
| **similar_industries** | *Industry Ecosystem Analyst* – researches related and similar industries (adjacent, upstream, downstream, complementary, competitive), comparing size, growth, and trends. |
| **distribution_channel** | *Distribution Channel & Logistics Analyst* – examines primary, retail, digital, and B2B distribution channels, their market‑share, growth, profitability, and logistics/supply‑chain aspects. |
| **financial_data** | *Financial & Economic Analyst* – gathers comprehensive financial data: industry revenue, profit metrics, financial ratios, investment activity, and economic impact, with sources and context. |
| **cost_structure** | *Cost Structure Analyst* – analyzes fixed and variable costs, cost drivers, and provides example breakdowns (e.g., rent, salaries, inventory, marketing). |
| **industry_revenue** | *Industry Revenue Analyst* – evaluates revenue streams (product sales, services, subscriptions), ranks industry profitability, and assesses revenue atomization among competitors. |
| **supply_demand_data** | *Supply & Demand Economics Analyst* – analyzes demand levels, trends, drivers, elasticity, and supply capacity, constraints, growth, and market equilibrium. |
| **key_statistics** | *Key Statistics Specialist* – collects quantitative market statistics (size, growth, demographics, performance metrics) with citations and detailed context. |
| **key_statistics_business** | *Business Count Specialist* – provides the latest estimate of the number of competing businesses in a U.S. industry, prioritizing sources like Gartner, IbisWorld, Statista, and U.S. Census. |
| **industry_structure** | *Industry Structure & Organization Analyst* – evaluates market concentration (HHI, CR4/CR8), competition level, barriers to entry, life‑cycle stage, structural characteristics, and value‑chain structure. |
| **external_drivers** | *External Factors & Drivers Analyst* – identifies and quantifies economic, technological, social/demographic, regulatory/political, environmental, and industry‑specific drivers affecting the industry. |
| **new_players** | *New Entrants & Innovation Tracker* – researches recent entrants, startups, market entry strategies, expansion patterns, incumbent responses, and funding/activity of new players. |
| **swot_analyzer** | *Strategic SWOT Analysis Expert* – synthesizes all gathered data into a detailed SWOT analysis (strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, threats) with supporting evidence and quantified impact. |
| **market_analyzer** | *Business Opportunity Identification Specialist* – analyzes the compiled data to pinpoint specific business opportunities across various service provider types, estimating market size, revenue potential, entry requirements, and strategic recommendations. |
