# Acme Employee Onboarding Handbook

## 1. First Week

### 1.1 Day-One Checklist
- Pick up laptop and badge at the lobby.
- Sign the offer letter and tax forms via the HR portal.
- Complete the mandatory security-awareness module.
- Join your team Slack channel.

### 1.2 Buddy Programme
Every new hire is assigned a buddy who answers day-to-day questions
during the first 90 days. Your buddy is listed on your welcome email.

## 2. Time Off

### 2.1 Vacation
Full-time employees accrue 20 vacation days per year, prorated from
the hire date. Requests are filed through the HR portal at least
two weeks in advance for stretches longer than two days.

### 2.2 Sick Leave
Up to 10 paid sick days per year. No advance notice is required, but
HR must be informed by 09:00 on the day of absence.

### 2.3 Parental Leave
12 weeks fully paid for the primary caregiver, 6 weeks for the
secondary caregiver. Notify HR at least 30 days in advance when
possible.

## 3. Benefits

### 3.1 Health Insurance
Coverage starts on day one. Choose between Basic, Plus, and Premium
plans in the benefits portal during your first 30 days.

### 3.2 Retirement Plan (401k)
Acme matches 100% of employee contributions up to 5% of salary.
Vesting is immediate.

### 3.3 Learning Budget
Each employee has a $1,500 annual learning budget for courses,
conferences, and books. File reimbursement through the expense portal.

## 4. Workplace Policies

### 4.1 Remote Work
Acme operates on a hybrid model: two days in office per week. Teams
choose the in-office days; coordinate with your manager.

### 4.2 Code of Conduct
Read the full Code of Conduct in the policies portal. Key points:
treat colleagues with respect, disclose conflicts of interest, never
share confidential customer data outside the company.
