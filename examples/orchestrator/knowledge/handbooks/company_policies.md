# Acme Company Policies (Expense & Finance)

## Expense Reimbursement

All expense reimbursements require a digital receipt attached to the
expense report in the finance portal. Receipts must clearly show:
vendor name, amount, currency, date, and last four digits of the
payment card.

Reimbursement caps:
- Meals during travel: $75 per day, breakfast and dinner only.
- Hotel: $250 per night within the United States, $300 outside.
- Ground transport: actual cost; ride-share preferred over taxi.
- Office supplies (working from home): $300 per year.

Submissions older than 60 days are rejected unless the manager
provides a written exception.

## Travel

Bookings must be made through the corporate travel portal. Direct
bookings on airline or hotel sites are not reimbursable except for
last-minute itinerary changes (less than 24 hours notice) — keep the
confirmation email as evidence.

Business-class flights are allowed for international segments longer
than six hours and require manager approval in advance.

## Corporate Cards

Corporate cards are issued to managers and frequent travellers. Card
transactions appear automatically in the expense portal — attach the
receipt within five business days.

Personal use is strictly forbidden. Accidental personal charges must
be repaid within 30 days; repeated occurrences trigger card revocation.

## Procurement

Any software or SaaS purchase above $5,000 per year requires Security
and Legal review before procurement raises a PO. Submit the request
through the procurement portal at least 10 business days in advance.

Free trials of SaaS products are permitted but must not be used with
production data — use a sandbox account.

## Reporting Fraud

Suspected fraud — duplicate invoices, vendor impersonation, internal
expense abuse — must be reported to fraud@acme.example. Reports are
confidential and investigated by Internal Audit.
